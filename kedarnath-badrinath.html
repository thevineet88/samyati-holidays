Package Overview

The Ultimate Char Dham Yatra

This 11-day spiritual journey takes you through the divine highlands of Uttarakhand, from the Ganga Aarti at Haridwar to the sacred Kedarnath temple perched at 11,755 ft, and the ancient Badrinath shrine on the banks of the Alaknanda river. We handle every detail, train tickets, accommodation, local transfers, permits, so you travel with complete peace of mind.

Departing from Mumbai by train, this group tour is perfect for first-time pilgrims and seasoned travellers alike. Our experienced guide ensures darshan at the right times, manages crowds, and takes care of every concern along the way.

Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

Board the train from Mumbai station in the evening. Our team will be present at the platform to assist with boarding and seat arrangements. Overnight journey begins.
Comfortable travel through central India. Get to know your fellow group members, rest, and prepare for the journey ahead. Meals on your own on the train.
Arrive Haridwar in the afternoon. Check into hotel, freshen up. Evening visit to Har Ki Pauri for the legendary Ganga Aarti, an experience you will never forget. Dinner and overnight at hotel.
Early morning departure. Brief stop at Rishikesh, Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula. Then onward drive through scenic Himalayan roads to Joshimath (6,150 ft). Dinner and overnight stay.
Drive to Badrinath (10,270 ft). Darshan at Badrinath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, the most revered of the four Char Dham shrines. Visit Mana Village (last Indian village before Tibet), Vasudhara Falls viewpoint. Return to Joshimath for the night.
Drive to Gaurikund (6,578 ft), the base camp for the Kedarnath trek. Prepare for the next day's trek. Rest is strongly advised. Our guide will brief you on the trek route, dos and don'ts. Dinner and early overnight stay.
The highlight of the entire trip. 4 AM start, 14 km trek to Kedarnath (11,755 ft) or helicopter option (additional charge). Darshan at Kedarnath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas. Overnight stay at Kedarnath guest house. This is an unforgettable day.
Morning puja at Kedarnath, then trek back down to Gaurikund. Drive to Rudraprayag, the sacred confluence of the Alaknanda and Mandakini rivers. Hotel check-in, dinner, overnight.
Drive to Rishikesh. Optional white-water rafting on the Ganga (additional charge, highly recommended). Evening visit to Beatles Ashram area and Triveni Ghat aarti. Overnight in Rishikesh.
Morning sightseeing in Rishikesh, yoga ashrams, cafes on the ghats. Drive to Haridwar. Shopping time at Har Ki Pauri market. Board return train to Mumbai in the evening.
Arrive Mumbai with memories that will last a lifetime. Tour ends. We hope to see you again on the next Samyati adventure!

Gokarna – Murudeshwar

Package Overview

Sun, Sand & Spirituality on the Karnataka Coast

Gokarna is Karnataka's best-kept secret, a laid-back coastal town with some of the cleanest, most scenic beaches in India. Hidden coves, dramatic headlands, and the iconic Om Beach that's shaped like the sacred symbol. Then add the towering 123-foot Shiva statue at Murudeshwar overlooking the Arabian Sea, and you have a trip that feeds both the soul and the senses.

Perfect for first-timers and repeat travellers alike. Great for friend groups, couples, and anyone who's been meaning to explore beyond Goa. We depart from Mumbai by overnight train so you wake up fresh in Karnataka.

Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

Board the overnight train from Mumbai in the evening. Our team assists with platform coordination. Sleep comfortably as the train takes you south towards Karnataka's coastline.
Arrive at Gokarna Road station in the morning. Transfer to hotel, freshen up, have breakfast. Afternoon, Mahabaleshwara Temple visit in Gokarna town. Evening at Om Beach, watch the sunset from this crescent-shaped wonder. Dinner and overnight stay.
The highlight day, boat or trek to Gokarna's hidden beaches: Kudle Beach (great for swimming), Half Moon Beach (secluded cove), and Paradise Beach (the most remote). Packed lunch included. Optional water sports at Om Beach in the evening. Sunset and dinner by the sea.
Drive ~95 km to Murudeshwar. Visit the iconic Murudeshwar Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, climb the gopuram for a stunning panoramic view of the Arabian Sea. Stand before the 123-foot Shiva statue, the tallest in the world after Sursagar. Visit Murudeshwar Fort (colonial-era ruins on a cliff). Return to Gokarna by evening. Farewell dinner.
Morning at leisure, one last stroll on the beach, breakfast, checkout. Transfer to station. Board return train to Mumbai. Arrive Mumbai the next morning with salty air memories and a great tan.

How It All Started

From a Group of Friends to 30,000+ Happy Travellers

Samyati Holidays started the way many good things do, out of a genuine need. Our founder, based in Airoli, Mumbai, kept noticing the same problem: friends wanting to travel together but nobody willing to handle the organizing. Hotels, trains, local transport, sightseeing, permits, the logistics are overwhelming when you're doing it alone.

So we stepped in. Our first group trip was to Kedarnath in 2019, 14 people, a borrowed tempo traveller, and a lot of learning on the go. That group came back safe, happy, and full of memories. And they told their friends.

Six years and 30,000+ travellers later, Samyati has become Mumbai's go-to for group tours. We run spiritual pilgrimages, mountain adventures, and coastal escapes, always with the same personal touch that started with that first Kedarnath trip.

We're still a small, tight-knit team. We know our travellers by name, not by booking number. And we believe that's what makes the difference.
